Dr. Sivanthi Aditanar College of Engineering

Established in 1995, Dr. Sivanthi Aditanar College of Engineering is committed to providing quality education to students, especially from rural areas, with an emphasis on analytical and practical skills.

Our college offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs, fostering an environment of academic excellence and innovation.

Programs Offered

  • B.E. in Computer Science and Engineering
  • B.E. in Electrical and Electronics Engineering
  • B.E. in Mechanical Engineering
  • B.Tech. in Information Technology
  • M.E. in VLSI Design
  • Master of Business Administration (MBA)
